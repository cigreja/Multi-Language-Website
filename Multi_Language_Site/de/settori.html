Agriculture, Fishing, Industry, Environment

Qualified assistance in highly-regulated sectors

The Firm provides highly-qualified assistance in the field of agriculture, fisheries, environment. Such assistance often involves dealing with public administrations and agencies in the interpretation and application of EU law. In many cases, issues can be resolved at this stage without embarking on a path of litigation. At the same time, the number and importance of the cases argued before the courts of the European Union has been very substantial.

Cases

To give a better idea of ​​the type of cases represented by the Firm before the Tribunal and the Court of Justice of the EU, we list, as examples, a few "direct" procedures:

  • action for annulment of a decision by the Commission by which an investment project for the rationalization of the production of dehydrated alfalfa had been excluded from Community financing ;
  • Action against the rejection, by the Commission, of a bid in a tender for the sale of Community barley held by the Austrian authority;
  • actions to challenge the decision by the Commission to deem illegal some regional aid provided to reduce the environmental impact of steel plants.

As for the cases argued before the Court of Justice as a result of preliminary questions raised by the national courts, we list:

- Preliminary references requested by the Italian courts:

  • on the interpretation of Community legislation on improving the efficiency of the agriculture entreprises regarding the aid scheme for the extensification of production of beef and veal meat;
  • on the interpretation and validity of the Community legislation on milk quotas;

- Preliminary references requested by the French courts:

  • on the correct interpretation of the Community legislation and international rules on protection of lake water from industrial pollution;
  • on a regulation of the Council of the European Union, which prohibits the use of drift nets for fishing tonno Mediterranean, deemed invalid.

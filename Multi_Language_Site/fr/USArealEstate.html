Real Estate in the USA & Germany

The Firm offers legal advice and assistance in real estate transactions in the USA and especially in New York, Miami and Houston. In particular, the Firm provides the following services:

  • preparation of any leases
  • assistance in the course of the contractual relationship

The object of a real estate transaction can be one of the following:

  • purchase of apartment or house for personal purposes
  • purchase of real estate property for investment
  • lease
  • commercial buildings or premises
